Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For large events like weddings, we recommend booking 9–12 months in advance. For corporate or smaller social events, 3–6 months is usually sufficient.

Do you offer day-of coordination?

Yes! While we love full-service planning, we offer "Month-of" packages for those who have planned their own event but want professional management on the big day.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer both flat-fee packages and customized percentage-based pricing depending on the scale and complexity of your event.
